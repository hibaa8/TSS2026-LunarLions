SUITS CAPCOM 00:00:00 ● Connecting

Welcome to the TSS (telemetry stream server) for the NASA SUITS challenge! This interface is where you can monitor and control various aspects of the mission and trigger scenarios for testing/debugging your software. You'll be connecting to this server to fetch telemetry values when building your rover or EVA interface. This website is what will be used by the moderators of the challenge during test week to track your EVA or rover activities and initiate error scenarios. Some helpful links:
